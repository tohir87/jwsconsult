About Us

At Solid-Link Consulting, we are passionate about matching students who wishes to study abroad with the right overseas institution that best suit their career’s aspiration in a safe, fun and friendly environment.Solid-Link Consulting specialises in counselling and recruitment of Nigerian students wishing to study abroad in leading Universities and Colleges in Europe.

Our chief consultant is an individual who resides and work as a lecturer in Ireland, therefore, has a wealth of first-hand experience and extensive knowledge of the higher education sector in Ireland and Nigeria.We offer our clients career counselling and guidance this allows them to make informed choices about courses available.Our partner colleges in Europe has a variety of quality courses and this makes it easier for the student to be placed in well-suited undergraduate and postgraduate courses.

We assist our clients with visa application and support clients who wishes to go through their own visa application themselves.

We also support students in applying for and securing suitable accommodation we provide guidance and assistance in making flight reservations where necessary.

We offer pastoral care to students and remain accessible to clients and parents over the course of their children’s stay in college.
