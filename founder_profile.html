Meet the founder

Olaniyi Kolawole

Founder

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Google+
  • LinkedIn

Olaniyi Kolawole is a Nigerian, resident in Ireland. He is a graduate of University College Cork (UCC), Ireland. He previously worked with the International Education Office of UCC in the area of International Students Recruitment in Africa. Presently, he lectures both undergraduates and postgraduate students in the Department of Sociology and School of Applied Social Studies at UCC. He also, besides, having extensive knowledge of the Higher Education Sector in Ireland and Nigeria, has a passion for teaching. He is an accomplished conference and motivational speaker, an experienced researcher, a fervent  believer in youth empowerment and economic independence via quality education.
